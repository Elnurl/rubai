### Full Description (≤4000 chars)
```
rubai is the AI execution coach that meets you where you are and walks you to where you want to be.

Tell rubai your goal — lose 10 kg, launch a side project, learn Spanish, run a marathon — and it asks exactly the right questions to understand your schedule, lifestyle, and obstacles. No generic advice. No cookie-cutter plans.

WHAT RUBAI DOES FOR YOU

• Tailored intake — rubai interviews you about your goal before building anything, so every plan fits your real life.
• Multi-phase roadmap — get a structured path broken into phases and milestones, with risk analysis built in.
• Daily action plan — each morning, a focused list of 3–7 tasks sized to your energy level and calendar.
• Adaptive coaching — talk to rubai any time. It remembers what you've said, tracks your progress, and adjusts the plan when life gets in the way.
• Voice input & image sharing — speak your check-ins hands-free or snap a photo of what you're working on.
• Calendar awareness — connect your calendar and rubai plans around your meetings automatically.
• Behavioral insights — rubai builds a behavioral profile over time so coaching gets smarter the longer you use it.

MULTIPLE GOALS, ONE COACH

Run several goals in parallel — fitness, career, learning — each with its own roadmap and daily plan. rubai keeps them separate and prioritised.

SUBSCRIPTION PLANS

• Free — one active goal, daily plan, and coach.
• Pro — unlimited goals, adaptive roadmap evolution, and voice coaching.
• Premium — everything in Pro plus behavioral profiling, advanced calendar sync, and priority AI responses.

rubai uses state-of-the-art AI (OpenAI GPT) to generate personalised plans and coaching, with automatic failover to Anthropic Claude so your coach is always available.

Your data is protected under GDPR. We never sell your data or use it to train AI models. Full privacy policy at: https://2cd42b33-105e-44b1-9128-46274a9215a3-00-1uq5b4opb5vic-1jdeiu28.riker.replit.dev/api/privacy
```
